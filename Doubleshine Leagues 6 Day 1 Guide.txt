Doubleshine Day 1 Guide v1.0
	Barbarian -> Woodsman -> Map -> Transmute -> Soul Harvest
	you can do others too have fun :)
	I am still undecided don't flame me because I went abundance

In general this route accounts for Barbarian Gathering and Endless Harvest to complete difficult gathering tasks early on. If you are rolling abundance you can 100% use this route but skilling tasks will take longer and I would recommend ditching them early if you don't need them. 

After Tier 2, the segments are largely independent and don't rely entirely on each other. You are welcome to move around between them as you see fit to avoid competition and train skills you would prefer doing. Do not force yourself to follow step by step and if you are stuck in a bottleneck just move on! i give you permission to not follow my guide its okay.

I am not accounting for Slayer, Herblore, Farming, Runecrafting but if you have seeds you should start farming it's good.
